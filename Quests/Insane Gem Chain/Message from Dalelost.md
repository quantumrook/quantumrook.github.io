## Insane Gem Quest Chain

### Objective

- Search [[Dalelost]] for a door marked with an arcane sigil.
> _Adventurer! Heed my call! I am in need of your services. Head east towards Dalelost and search for a sigil marking a door. That is where I reside. I have a proposition of great interest for you._
