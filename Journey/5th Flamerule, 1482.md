# 5th Flamerule, 1482

## Deep [[Ankhwood]]

The two press on, northward, for four hours without interruption, but halt as they enter the diseased portion of the wood. [[Quinn]] leans over to [[Leshanna]] in a whisper, "We're about an hours travel away from the Monastery." She nods in response and the two continue traveling.

		Group Stealth Check:
			Q: 16, L: 20
			Group mod = +8
		Group's passive stealth is 21 (Lowest + Mod)

The two, getting used to each other's habits, begin to move more stealthily through the wood, with Quinn taking the lead and Leshanna fanned out and moderately covering their tracks.

		Encounter Check: 2, None.
		
And within the hour, approach the monastery. Quinn crouches down as he waits for Leshanna to catch up. "I don't like how quiet it has been on this leg of the journey" he whispers.

## The [[Diseased Monastery]]

		Encounter Check: 4, None.

"Agreed." Quinn draws his weapons as he leads them into the small clearing before the thicket and the previous goblin encounter. Finding no signs of activity outside, he leads her through the overgrowth and to the still open stone door. He pauses for a moment, taking two rags from his pack and wraps one about his face. Leshanna follows suit and the two enter the Foray.

		Encounter Check: 19, Yes.
		Dungeon #83: Within a pool, a flawless gem shimmers.

		Fungus Check (w/ Adv):
		Q & L pass.

> Q: Are the corpses still present.
> A: Maybe. Keywords: Curse, Forbidden, Wary

> Q: Does the fungal infection have zombie like powers? (Likely)
> A: Yes

Quinn halts halfway into the room, seeing nothing. "There should be corpses here..." He lights a sun rod and slides it across the floor into the bathroom.

> Q: Are there zombies in the backroom?
> A: No

Silence hangs in the air for a long moment after the rod finishes skittering across the rough stone floor. The light reveals long shadows of the bathroom but no figures, and after an eternity, nothing comes out from the room. The two cautiously approach the room and Leshanna spots a shimmer out of the corner of her eye. In the farthest corner, under a pool of water, sits a [[Insane Gem|glimmering flawless gem]], glowing a soft purple. She holds a hand outstretched pointing Quinn to it.

He looks about the room one more time before grabbing the sun rod and moving closer.

		Quinn, Perception Check: 16
		The gem appears to be normal.

> Q: Does anything bad happen when he picks it up?
> A: Yes.

Quinn sighs, knowing that [[Denal]] might be interested in the gem's appearance, and scoops it up, putting it into his bag. He turns around to explain to Leshanna when he notices a figure behind her, emitting a soft purple glow from its neck and face. He quickly throws the sun rod at the figure, and to his horror, sees the blackblade he had killed not just 3 days before standing behind his new companion.

		Enter Intiative
		Deadly Encounter
		3 Zombies, 4 Rotters, 1 Corruption Corpse
		
Quinn rushes around the tubs, flanking the zombie, and strikes deftly into the zombie's back. Leshanna spins around and disappears from sight, reappearing in the Foray. Her staff crackles with lightning as three bolts shoot out, two hitting their target and the third missing. The zombie next to Quinn tries to smack him but moves too slow for the rogue. Another zombie moves forward from the Foray and reaches out to grab Quinn and succeeds. A third zombie, attracted to Leshanna due to the lightning, moves after her and swipes at the Eladrin, missing. From the stairs, an orb of necrotic filth flies past Leshanna, as she ducks and the madden mage's corpse enters the foray.

Three other zombies move to swarm her, and she manages to fend off two of the attacks before getting caught by the third. She lets out a yelp of pain from the impact and Quinn tries to break free from his grasp - noting that they've been cut off from each other. Unsuccessful, he stabs at the blackblade, hoping to thin the numbers and decapitates the corpse.

Leshanna steps back and holds her staff high, glowing a bright blue and slams it into the ground. The blue light radiates outward in a wave, burning the goblin cutters to ash and lighting the sturdier zombies on fire. The dead mage hurls another ball of necrotic energy at the invoker, again missing. The goblin swordsman holding Quinn swipes at his mask, tearing it from his face.

Quinn uses the momentum from the strike to break the grip, steps back, and with a sly flourish, strikes the zombie with his blade and sets up a riposte. Leshanna, determined to end the mage, holds her staff out towards him and a beam of blue radiant light shoots forth but misses the mage as she ducks to avoid another orb of necrotic energy. The swordsman swipes at Quinn but misses as the rogue cuts into its arm.

Slipping on the still wet stone floor, Quinn tilts off balance as he tries a follow up swing. Leshanna releases another beam of light, this time connecting with the mage and burning him with more radiant light. The mage, flaying about, looses another orb which the eldarin easily dodges. The swordsman misses again, swiping furiously for the rogue.

Eager to end this, Quinn swipes at the zombie's neck and his blade sticks, not completely cutting through. Leshanna releases one last beam of light, causing the mage to crumple to the ground as the flames burn away everything but the skeleton. The remaining zombie, acting as if it had a sense of preservation, swipes at Quinn's hand, cutting into it. A yelp of pain escapes Quinn, before he pushes his hand forward, finishing the cut.

The two retreat back into the corner of the foray where Quinn coughs before quickly digging through his pack to find fabric to make a new mask out of. They take turns watching the staircase where the zombies emerged from while the other rests and recovers. After catching their breath, they slowly stand up and move towards the staircase, picking up the sun rod. 

## The [[Fungal Caverns]]

> Oracle: This area is home to a mining operation. Keywords: Faith, Disciple, Device
> The mining operation is being done by the cult in search of a device related to Vecna's interests.

The two exchange looks as they approach the stairs, getting a better look: this staircase is more of a hole carved out of the structure with crude stairs leading down, implying that this was roughly added sometime after the monastery's construction, and most likely, its abandonment.

		Stealth Checks:
		Q 12, L 11

Leshanna holds up a hand, telling Quinn to stop before the progress much further and she heads down the spiraling staircase and sees a strong purple glow coming from the room ahead. Returning just a few short moments later, she points to the sun rod and shakes her head no. Quinn carefully places the rod down, wedging it into a corner, so it doesn't roll away before following Leshanna down the stairs.

At the bottom, the two see a brightly lit cavern, bathed in a warm purple light. Mining equipment is scattered about the area, implying this has been going on for sometime but there are no sounds of active activity in the cavern. Taking a moment to inspect their surroundings, they note the glow is coming from crystal growths climbing the walls of the cavern. The two share a look of displeasure before readying their weapons and pressing forward into the cavern.

> Q: Is there anything else down here?
> A: Maybe. Stray, Foot, Possession
> If the party are holding torches, or there are torches or candles present in the area, something blows them all out suddenly.

The cavern glows and then shuts into pitch darkness before slowly brightening to a dim level. A spectral figure stands 60 feet in front of the two and points towards Quinn. Behind the spectre, at the back edge of the cavern, they see two figures dressed in mining equipment slowly rise from laying on the ground. Upstairs, Quinn's bag is glowing a bright purple from the gem he picked up earlier.

Quinn quickly spins, drawing his shuriken and looses it towards the spectre. A loud crack echoes through the cavern as the shuriken slams into the spectre, knocking it backwards onto the floor. The two figures advance, and upon getting closer, Leshanna lets out a harsh whisper: "Skeletons!" The spectre staggers as it stands on its feet before shimmering and disappearing from sight and a loud thud is heard right in front of the two as some dust is kicked up.

Leshanna quickly steps back, raising her staff, lighting coursing about her before shooting out in three bolts: one at the source of the sound, and the other two toward the skeletons in the back. The first bolt strikes the air in front of Quinn, illuminating the spectre for the human's eyes. The other two bolts also effortlessly find their targets, shocking the skeletons.

Quinn, after a moment of surprise, deftly swings his shortsword at the spectre, cleaving it in two. The necrotic energy turns into a goo like substance as he cuts through, and the form shimmers before collapsing into a puddle. 

One of the skeletons rushes towards Leshanna, and the other Quinn. Swing its heavy longsword faster than Leshanna expected, it collides with her armor, knocking her back slightly. Her armor unleashes a bright blue radiant light in response, burning the skeleton and forcing it back temporarily. Leshanna quickly regains her posture and raises her staff again, channeling divine radiance at her attacker, burning it to ash.

As the other skeleton charges towards Quinn, he throws his shuriken at the knee joint, causing it to stumble as it comes into range, and its longsword swings wide. In the background, Leshanna sees two zombies quickly shuffling around from a turn in the cavern towards the two adventurers. As the close the distance, she focuses her divine invocations on the skeleton attacking Quinn, striking it with radiant fire. It opens its mouth as if to scream in pain, but no noise comes forth, which is almost a more disturbing sight than if it had made a noise.

Quinn, having set up the skeleton to be off balance, swings his blade with a sly flourish, cutting into the mass of clothing and bones and it collapses into a pile of dust at his feet. While Leshanna was busy focusing on the skeleton, she failed to notice the zombie quietly close the distance as it swipes at her, slamming into her chainmail. Backing up against the cavern wall, she quickly begins to channel more divine fire at her attacker, smiting it instantly as the flames roar with a fierce intensity. The second zombie advances on Quinn, swiping away, but the rogue expertly dodges the slow lumbering attack.

As he ducks, he swipes with his sword, connecting with the undead mass and positions himself for quick follow up. Leshanna quickly moves to flank the zombie, cracking its skull with her staff, and Quinn, seeing his chance, follows up with his riposte as the zombie swipes at him again. Battered by attacks on both sides, the zombie can't seem to catch the rogue with its grasp and so turns to face Leshanna. Quinn, seeing an even bigger opening quickly cuts into the mass, and the corpse falls down to the ground, lifeless once more.

The two search the rest of the cavern, cautiously, but find no other corpses or anything of note. The crystals begin to return to their previous brightness as the two finish their search. Finally feeling safe enough to speak freely, Quinn breaks the silence, "Okay, what the hell was that?"

"Some sort of spectre that seemed to command the dead."

"Did you see any crystal growths or whatnot on them?"

"I did not, you?"

"No, unlike our previous set of company." Quinn says looking about the cavern again and shudders. "I don't like what that implies either, we should leave - our work here is done. Denal can send better equipped people to investigate this area, if she wishes."

"What was with that gem earlier? Why did you pick it up?" Leshanna asks as the two begin making their way back to the crude staircase.

"I figured it might be important.. It seemed to match the glow of this, and the growth I had seen on the madden mage. I guess I should've asked you if it seemed cursed or not, but I guess its a bit late for that." Quinn shudders again, "I hope to never see this place ever again or those crystals."

## The [[Diseased Monastery]], Foray

Leshanna nods as the two climb the stairs, and Quinn retrieves the sun rod, leading them to their packs. Unknown to the two, the gem had stopped glowing brightly when the spectre was felled. Quinn looks to the doorway, discarding the mostly spent sun rod, "Hopefully we haven't been in here too long, I'd like to put some decent distance between us and here before nightfall."

> Weather check: 

"Agreed," Leshanna says, stepping out into the warmer air and removing her cloth mask. The midday sun, cloaked behind clouds, still offered far more warmth than the damp monastery. "We should either burn these or leave them." She says to Quinn as he undoes his mask. He nods, taking hers and setting them on the ground. He goes to light them with some flint and steel before Leshanna puts a hand on his shoulder.

"Ah right, radiant fire would probably be best," he says as he backs up and watches as Leshanna channels the power of [[Ioun]] once more to bring a small flame to the cloth. He looks about the clearing, seeing nothing new of note before heading towards the way out.

"Are we heading straight back or swinging by Dalelost?" Leshanna asks.

Quinn hesitates for a moment, thinking about how nice it would be to be back at the [[Rusty Chain]] for a decent break before sighing. "As much as I would love to regal [[Palmer]] and Denal with our tale, I suppose its best to return with an empty checklist..." the rogue begrudges as he turns southwest, leading them away from [[Ankhapur]] and towards Dalelost.
